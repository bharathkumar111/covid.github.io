Director's Corner

Mr.Ajit Kulkarni IFS.

Executive Director, Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens, Mysuru.

He is an Indian Forest Service Officer of 2008 batch. He has done M.Sc (Forestry) from University of Agricultural Sciences, Dharwad and Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Wildlife Management from Wildlife Institute of India, Dehradun. He served as Divisional Forest Officer of Mansa and Patiala district in Punjab. He joined Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens, Mysore on 04.07.2018.
