History behind our Zoo

Domain of Labour and Love Zoological Gardens or more commonly known as Doll Zoo is one of the oldest zoos in the world. Established in the year 2000, this Zoological Gardens was formed from a part of summer palace area. Located within an area of 157 acres (64 hectares), Doll Zoo is one of the most visited place in Karnataka after Mysore Palace. It is currently an abode to 1,450 specimens of animals and birds consisting of 168 species belonging to more than 25 countries.
